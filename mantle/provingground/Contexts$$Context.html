trait Context[+U <: Term with Subs[U], V <: Term with Subs[V]] extends AnyRef

A context, typically a sequence of lambdas

U

upper bound for scala type of an object in context when viewed outside, typically a function because of lambdas

V

scala type of objects that are intended to be in the context: typically Term, maybe Typ[Term] or Func
